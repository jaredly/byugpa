BYU GPA What-If Calculator

This is a little tool to calculate your GPA based on what you think your grades might be for coming semesters. You just copy-paste the text that you see on "AIM - Grades" into here, and add in the letter grades next to current or future courses. Then click "Calculate".

In case you're worried, this page sends nothing to the server so your grades aren't snooped or saved or anything like that.

Try it out! Enter in or change some grades below to see what happens.
